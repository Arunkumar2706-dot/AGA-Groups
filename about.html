Our Story

Building Excellence Across Generations

AGA Groups is built on a strong foundation of trust, craftsmanship, and commitment. With over 35 years of experience in the construction industry and more than 8 years of success in real estate development since 2019, we have been helping families and investors turn their aspirations into reality.

Our journey began with a passion for creating quality spaces that stand the test of time. Over the years, we have expanded our expertise from construction services to premium real estate developments, delivering projects that combine modern infrastructure, strategic planning, and lasting value.

Every project we undertake reflects our dedication to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Whether it's constructing dream homes, developing residential communities, or creating investment opportunities, we approach each endeavor with the same commitment to excellence that has defined us for decades.

Today, AGA Groups continues to shape communities, build meaningful relationships, and create spaces where people can live, grow, and prosper with confidence.

  • 35+ Years of Construction Excellence.
  • 8+ Years of Real Estate Development.
  • One Vision: Building Trust, Creating Communities.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class construction solutions that exceed client expectations through innovation, integrity, and sustainable building practices. We strive to create spaces that enhance lives and communities while maintaining the highest standards of safety and craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To be India's most respected construction company, recognized for creating iconic landmarks that stand the test of time. We envision a future where every AGA Groups project sets new benchmarks in design, sustainability, and engineering excellence.
